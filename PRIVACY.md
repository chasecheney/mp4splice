# MP4Splice Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-14_

MP4Splice does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

- **No data collection.** The app has no analytics, no telemetry, and no accounts.
- **No network access.** MP4Splice does not connect to the internet. Your videos never leave your Mac.
- **Local files only.** The app reads and writes only the files and folders you explicitly choose
  through the system open/save dialogs, and processes them entirely on your device.

Because no data is collected, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete on our side.
